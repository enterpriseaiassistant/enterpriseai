Onboarding
Assistant

A guided onboarding prototype that walks users through setup steps, prevents missed tasks, and delivers a consistent experience at scale.

📋

Overview

Onboarding Assistant is a prototype designed to help users move through onboarding steps more easily by providing guided, step-by-step assistance. It reduces confusion, minimises missed tasks, and makes the onboarding experience consistent regardless of who is providing support.

⚠️

Current Problem

!

Users often don't know the right sequence of onboarding steps, leading to confusion and delays early in the process.

!

Important setup tasks are easily missed or delayed without structured guidance, causing downstream issues.

!

Onboarding quality varies depending on who provides support — there's no consistent, repeatable experience.

✅

How It Helps

✓

Guides users step by step through every onboarding task, ensuring the right actions happen in the right order.

✓

Reduces dependency on manual back-and-forth communication between users and support teams.

✓

Delivers a consistent and clear onboarding journey for every user, independent of who is supporting them.
